About Me:


My name is Artur Jasinski.

In 2013 I decided to move from Poland to United Kingdom with my family. From 7 years Bedford is our hometown. There was few reasons why I moved. Opportunity to learn English language, first job, better salary. I started my work career in surrounding Bedford warehouses. Very quickly I understood that my potential is a lot higher than performing employers targets as pulling pumptruck and picking products all day. Few weeks later I decided to invest in myself and I passed Reach Truck/ Counter balance exam, it gave me licence which allowed me to make my job easier and earn a bit more. I wasn't still proud of myself, truck become very easy to use in few months and I still had feeling that I again stopped improving myself and I'm just consuming time, then my manager decided to give me chance. I got promoted to warehouse coordinator. This role was exciting me. I had finally possibility to improve my English and window for new opportunities, which I never think about before, as for example Manager position. Each day brought me new challenges and responsibilities. I started to feeling that I finally mean something for someone, but I wasn't fully happy of this position. My manager gave me priceless advice "Artur maybe try coding" as he started it recently too. I agreed, and tested it, then decided that this is what I want to do for rest of my life. I don't want to stop, I want to improve and prove it!
